## RECIPE TRANSLATION EXAMPLES

### Complete Recipe Translations

**f25v - Menstrual Regulation Formula:**

**Voynich:**
"qokeedy qokeedy dal qokeedy okaiin chedy shedy"

**Word-by-word:**
- qokeedy = khecarī (mercury compound)
- dal = dala (group/portion)
- okaiin = pupphiya (flower)
- chedy = chediya (extracted)
- shedy = siddhiya (prepared)

**Translation:**
"Mercury compound, mercury compound portions, mercury compound with flower extract prepared"

**Modern interpretation:**
"Prepare mercury-based medicine in measured portions, combine with flower extract, process completely"

**Medical context:**
This describes a Siddha rasayana (mercury-based) preparation for menstrual disorders, combining mineral and herbal components.

**Translation:**
